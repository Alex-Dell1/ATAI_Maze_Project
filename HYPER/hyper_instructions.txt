Instructions how to run the HYPER files.

Consult the following files in prolog:

hyper.pl
util_ilp.pl
parameters.pl

ilp_task_adjacent.pl for predicate "adjacent" or
ilp_task_move.pl for predicate  "move" or
ilp_task_reach.pl for predicate "reach" or
ilp_task_move_reach.pl for combined learning of "move" and "reach"

maze.pl for the 5x5 maze or
maze7.pl for the 7x7 maze or
maze9.pl for the 9x9 maze

Make sure the parameters are set as specified in the report.
